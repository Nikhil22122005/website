1. The generation of current in this circuit is too low, as the smaller Piezoelectric Plates are used (for demonstration purpose)

2. If the LED bulb might not be visible in      broad daylight, Kindly use the 'Multimeter'
to see the generation of voltage in circuit.

   ----------------------------------------------------------------------__________________________________________

Principle :-

1. Current is generated from the Piezoelectric Disc, consists of Pressure sensitive Quartz Crystals which generate current when Pressure is applied...

2. The amount of generation of current DEPENDS ON THE SIZE of piezoelectric discs you use .
___________________________________________----------------------------------------------------------------------

Uses :-

1. Nature Friendly and Free source of energy like SOLAR.

2. Pollution free Generation of energy.

3. Can be very useful, when practically applied at Crowded places like Railway Station, Foot-paths, Schools, Speed-Breakers, Temples ..etc
